About HealSpace

Welcome to HealSpace 🌿 — your safe digital corner for mental wellness. Our mission is to create a space where anyone can learn about their mental health, take insightful quizzes, and find gentle guidance toward healing — all without judgment or barriers.

HealSpace offers a Dashboard to explore different mental health conditions, a self-awareness Quiz to help you understand your emotions, and a comforting Chatbot named Healy 🤖 — a virtual companion designed to listen and provide emotional support whenever you need it.

We believe mental health should be accessible to everyone — that’s why HealSpace doesn’t require authentication or sign-up. Your privacy and comfort come first.
